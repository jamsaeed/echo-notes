About Echo Notes

Echo Notes is a passion project designed to help you capture your thoughts and ideas in a fun and interactive way. With secure authentication, a cozy sticky note design, and a built-in text-to-speech feature, your notes are always accessible and engaging.

Purpose

To provide a friendly and secure environment for journaling your thoughts digitally.

Features

Create, read, update, delete, and listen to your notes anywhere, anytime.

Future Plans

Deploy as a full app with more advanced features and share it on App Stores.

Design

Inspired by sticky notes and handwriting fonts to create a warm, cozy feel.
